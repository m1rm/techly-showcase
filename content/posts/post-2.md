Hugo's built-in taxonomy support works out of the box with Techly. Add tags to
any post's front matter:

```yaml
tags = ['hugo', 'go', 'web']
```

Each tag gets its own archive page listing all matching posts. This makes it easy
for readers to discover related content across your blog.
